about

My name is Niels and I'm a university student at the IT-University of Copenhagen where I study Software Development and Technology and expect to finish my master degree June 2016, with the specialisation 'Business Analytics'.

During my education I've been exposed to a number of concepts within computer science. I have taken courses in discrete mathematics, algorithms and data structures, and data mining. See a complete list of courses I've taken further down. During the next semester I will be taking courses on Big Data Management and Scala, among others.

I have a bachelor degree in Business, Asian Language and Culture from Copenhagen Business School, where I studied Japanese and Japanese culture in particular. My bachelor project was written for a freight forwarding company in which we interviewed eployees and examined the relations with the asian affiliations. I focused on agency theory, which I still find very interesting and believe can be applied to many aspects of life.

I decided to persue a masters in software development because I have always been interested in computer programming, and have an interest in combining the fields of business and information technology. In the future, I would like to see myself become a connecting element between the two, which I think is vital for any modern business wanting to succeed. By following the business analytics specialisation I ensure a silver lining throughout my university education where I can combine the two areas.
